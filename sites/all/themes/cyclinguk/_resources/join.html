Whether you’re new to cycling, ride regularly or want to get back into it, Cycling UK caters for you. For over 135 years, Cycling UK, formerly CTC, has championed a simple belief – that cycling is a wonderful gift that should remain accessible and available to all.

Great reasons to join

£10m insurance cover

£10m third party liability insurance cover to protect you on and off road

The third party liability insurance cover is there to protect you from a claim made by a third party, on and off road. With an indemnity limit of £10m it offers invaluable cover when you need it the most. Worldwide except USA and Canada.

Rides and events

Local rides and events with over 13,000 rides every year

Whatever cycling you do, we have hundreds of local groups across the country. You’ll be able to join in and ride with other cyclists, discovering new places and making friends along the way. New members will be given a warm welcome and supported regardless of their cycling experience at our local groups. And if you’re interested in our campaigns and having a say on cyclists’ rights, there are opportunities to meet like-minded individuals through our campaigning groups.

'Cycle' magazine

Six issues of our award winning 'Cycle' magazine

Cycle gives a great insight into cycling. It is packed with varied and interesting ride features, campaign updates, routes and product reviews to inspire you and help you get the most from your cycling – full members receive the publication included within their membership.

Exclusive discounts

Discounts on bikes, cycling and outdoor leisure products

As a member of Cycling UK, you’ll benefit from a range of discounts offered through our commercial partners, such as Cotswold Outdoor and reduced ticket prices for cycle shows.

Professional advice

Bikes and cycling product advice

Expert reviews, features and advice from everyday cyclists.

Incident Line

Cycling UK Incident Line

The experienced legal claims team is here to advise you in the event of a cycling accident. Although an element we hope you won’t need, this is a valued part of our membership package.

And more!

All members also get...
  • Competitions and ideas to help you get the most from your cycling
  • CycleClips, our entertaining weekly e-newsletter, full of all the latest cycling news
  • For those who like to venture even further afield, our own holiday company offering a range of holidays and tours, tailored for cyclists
  • Access to specially tailored cycle theft insurance and specific cycling travel insurance

Help make a difference

By joining us you’ll be helping to give our campaigning team a louder voice for local and national cycling issues; plus your membership goes towards supporting our cycling projects to get thousands of people cycling every year.
Plus you can Gift Aid your Cycling UK membership if you are eligible. Gift Aid enables a charity to claim 25p for each £1 of your donation – so no additional cost to you, but means more money for our charitable work.

Why one of our members joined us

Because I ride a bike. I don’t tour, not now. I did in my youth, 30 years ago. I ride for pleasure. I ride a mountain bike. I ride to work. I ride for work. It seemed to be the organisation to support. I was a member in my youth when I was cycle touring. I rejoined five years ago when I came back to cycling.

Ian
